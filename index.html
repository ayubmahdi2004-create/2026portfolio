01 — UI/UX Design · Capstone

StepOut

A wellness app designed to help youth (ages 15–21) break cycles of indoor overstimulation and reclaim their day — one step at a time.

Problem: Students 15–21 spend too much time online and indoors, causing poor focus, poor sleep, and disconnection from the real world.

Solution: A free mobile app with daily outdoor tasks, progress tracking, journaling, and analytics — designed with a calm, nature-inspired visual language.

Tools: Figma, component design system, high-fidelity prototyping.

Interaction Design User Research Figma System Design Prototyping Usability Testing
02 — Concept Art & Illustration

Character Design

A growing collection of original character designs and concept illustrations spanning fantasy, sci-fi, manga, and anime aesthetics.

Range: Full-colour character illustrations, action compositions, intimate portraits, manga-style ink work, and gesture studies — demonstrating versatility across styles, moods, and techniques.

Approach: Each character begins from a concept or world prompt. Costume design, lighting, and colour palette are used to communicate personality and story without words.

Illustration Character Design World Building Digital Painting Manga / Linework Colour Theory
03 — 3D Environment Design

3D Backgrounds

A series of fully realised 3D background environments — from ancient citadels and stylised villages to painterly natural landscapes.

Scope: Architectural environments, fantasy landscapes, stylised nature scenes, and village streetscapes. Each background is designed to support foreground characters and narrative action.

Process: Modelled, lit, and rendered in 3D software with stylised post-processing. Colour grading and atmospheric effects are applied to achieve the final visual tone.
